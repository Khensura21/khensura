Who am I?

My name is Khensu-Ra (pronounced kenz-rah), but to many I go by Ra. I grew up in the best city in the world, Chicago (yea I said it), in the infamous neighborhood of Englewood. I love my home teams Chicago Bulls, Blackhawks, Bears, and the White Soxs of course!

After doing my undergraduate studies in Physics and Math at Morehouse College, I decided to join the dark-side...Computer Science (plays evil villian laugh)! Currently, I am a Master's student at NYU's Interactive Telecommunications Program (ITP) where I study as a developer, creative technologist, and researcher. My research explores intersections including Holistic Health, Technology + Education, and Sociopolitical Activism.

In my down time, I participate in several hobbies. These include breakin' ankles on the basketball court, making a new vegan dish, watching my favorite anime, or reading a good book. Everyday I strive to learn something new to better my craft. I hope to use my technology skills one day to continue to make the world a better place for mankind because a wise man once said, with great powers come great responsibilities!
